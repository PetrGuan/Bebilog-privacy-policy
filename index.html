Privacy Policy

We built the Bebilog app as a free app with in app purchase for advanced features. This SERVICE is provided by us and is intended for use as is.

This page is used to inform users regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decides to use our Service.

If you choose to use our Service, then you agree to the policies described on this page. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe experience.

Information Collection and Use

Bebilog is designed with a privacy-first architecture. All data you create — including baby profiles, growth records, feeding logs, sleep records, photos, and other activity data — is stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on external servers.

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services. No personally identifiable information is collected by us in any way.

Network Access

Bebilog works entirely offline for all daily recording features. Internet access is required only for the following purpose:

  • In-App Purchases: The app communicates with Apple's StoreKit servers to load product pricing and to process or restore purchases. This is handled entirely by Apple's secure infrastructure.

No user-created content (baby data, photos, records, etc.) is ever transmitted over the network.

App Permissions

Bebilog may request the following device permissions, each only when you initiate the related feature:

  • Photo Library: Requested only when you choose to select or save media within the app.
  • Camera: Requested only when you use the in-app camera to take photos or videos.
  • Microphone: Requested only when you record audio or video with sound.
  • Notifications: Requested only when you enable reminders for vaccines, medicine schedules, appointments, or pumping timers.

You can revoke or change any of these permissions at any time in your device's iOS Settings.

Cookies

Bebilog is a native iOS application and does not use cookies. The app does not embed any web content or third-party services that use cookies.

Data Security

Your data is protected by Apple's device-level encryption and iOS app sandboxing. All information exists only within the app's private container on your device and cannot be accessed by other apps. We do not have access to your data at any time.

Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • You can delete individual baby profiles and all associated records directly within the app.
  • Uninstalling Bebilog will permanently and irrevocably delete all local data stored by the app.

Children's Privacy

Bebilog is a baby tracking tool designed for use by parents and guardians. The app does not target or knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. All data entered into the app is provided by the parent or guardian and remains under their control on their own device.

Note that these external sites are not operated by us. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of those websites. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.
