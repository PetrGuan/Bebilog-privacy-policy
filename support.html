Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. Bebilog does not upload anything to cloud servers. Your baby's information never leaves your phone.

What happens if I delete the app?

Uninstalling Bebilog will permanently delete all data stored by the app. We recommend using the Export feature in Settings to back up your records before removing the app.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Open Bebilog → Settings → tap the upgrade banner → tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to iPhone Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → select Bebilog → Cancel Subscription. Your Pro features remain active until the end of the current billing period.

Can I track multiple babies?

Yes. You can add multiple baby profiles in Settings and switch between them at any time.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings → Data Management → Export Data. Your records will be exported as a CSV file that you can save, share, or open in any spreadsheet app.

Which languages are supported?

Bebilog is available in English, German, Spanish, French, Japanese, Korean, Russian, Simplified Chinese, and Traditional Chinese.

What iOS version is required?

Bebilog requires iOS 17.0 or later.
